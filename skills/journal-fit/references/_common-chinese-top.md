# Common Norms — Top Chinese Economics Journals (经济研究 · 管理世界 · 世界经济 · 经济学（季刊）)

Cross-cutting conventions shared by the top Chinese economics journals. These journals
are operated wholly or partly by **policy institutions** (CASS Institute of Economics;
the State Council's Development Research Center for 管理世界), so they reward work that
speaks to **national priorities** *and* meets rising econometric standards. Per-journal
files record what is **distinctive** (经济学季刊 is the outlier — most "Western";
管理世界 the most policy/strategy-driven). **Last updated:** 2026-05.

## What they share
- A paper should answer a **big, relevant question** — ideally tied to China's
  development / policy agenda (高质量发展, 共同富裕, 新发展格局, 中国式现代化, 双碳,
  数字经济, 统一大市场), grounded in China's **制度背景**.
- A rising methodological bar: causal identification (DID, IV, RD, event study), clean
  data, mechanism tests — converging toward English-journal standards while keeping the
  policy framing.
- A **中国问题 / 中国故事** identity: why this question matters in and for China.

## The near-FIXED empirical skeleton
Unlike English journals, Chinese empirical papers follow a recognized template, with
**numbered sections (一、二、三…)**:
1. **引言** — 现实背景与大问题; 制度背景; 研究问题; brief 文献定位; closing with an
   enumerated **边际贡献** ("本文的边际贡献主要体现在以下几个方面:第一……第二……
   第三……") and often 结构安排 (roadmap).
2. **文献综述 与/或 理论分析与研究假设** — derive explicit hypotheses 假设 H1, H2, …
3. **研究设计** — 模型设定 (the regression equation); 变量定义与测量; 数据来源与
   描述性统计.
4. **实证结果与分析** — 基准回归; 内生性处理 (工具变量 etc.).
5. **机制检验 / 影响渠道** (mechanisms).
6. **进一步分析** — 异质性分析, 调节效应 (heterogeneity).
7. **稳健性检验** (robustness) — often substantial.
8. **结论与政策启示** — a **substantial** section, not a throwaway.

Deviating from this skeleton reads as "not knowing the genre."

## 引言 (introduction) conventions
- Open with the real-world / national-policy 大背景, not a literature gap.
- Establish the 制度背景 and why China is the right or instructive setting.
- State the research question and approach.
- Close with an explicit, **enumerated 边际贡献** relative to existing work — expected,
  not optional.

## 研究假设 (hypotheses)
Empirical papers usually derive explicit, numbered hypotheses (H1, H2, …) from a
理论分析 section, then test them in order. Stating hypotheses explicitly is a genre
expectation (more so than in English journals).

## 摘要 / 关键词 / JEL
- **中文摘要** ~150–300 字: 问题—方法/数据—主要发现—含义; declarative. No equations.
- **English abstract (英文摘要)** usually required.
- **关键词** (3–5) and **JEL 分类号** are standard.

## 政策启示 (policy implications)
A real, substantial closing section — derive concrete, actionable 政策建议 from the
findings and connect them to national strategy. This is a **major differentiator** from
English journals and is weighted heavily by editors and referees: heaviest for 管理世界
and 经济研究, lighter for 经济学季刊.

## Literature & citations
- Cite **both 中文 and 外文** literature: show command of the relevant Chinese
  literature (and recent same-journal work) as well as the international frontier.
- In-text author–year; reference list in **GB/T 7714** style.

## Register & format
- Formal, 规范 academic Chinese; third person; measured tone.
- Quantitative / econometric emphasis.
- 表 (tables) and 图 (figures) with 注释; report 稳健标准误, significance stars, control
  sets, and fixed effects.
- Typical length ~15,000–25,000 字.

## Where the journals differ (see per-journal files)
- **经济研究** — flagship; broadest; highest theory + empirics bar; strong policy framing.
- **管理世界** — most policy/strategy-driven; 中国问题 front and center; management +
  economics; big questions; sometimes invited 笔谈.
- **世界经济** — open-economy / international / trade-finance emphasis.
- **经济学（季刊）** — the most "Western": methods-first, lighter policy framing, closest
  to an international field journal in structure and tone.
