**Generator (2026-06-23).** Table A2 (`tab:sf4_trades`, the observed strict-bilateral inter-district SWP transactions
in Fact 4) is built by **`program/3_exhibits/build_tab_sf4_trades.py`**, reading the canonical multi-source
transfer-price panel **`data/clean/swp_transfer_prices.csv`** (from build_swp_transfer_prices.py, \dref{swptransfer})
and filtering to **`classification == "spot_bilateral_strict"` & `year in {2014, 2015}`** → the 21 transactions
(3 buyers Belridge/Berrenda/Lost Hills, 7 sellers, total 27,597 AF). Per-AF price is the panel's `price_usd_af`;
rows grouped by buyer, ordered by year then volume desc. Writes `latex/tables/sf4_observed_trades.tex`.
PIPELINE_manual Exhibit 8.

**Why this is the source, not the old file.** The committed table was a hand-edited artifact of the now-archived
`program/sandbox/build_stylized_facts_visuals.py`, which read the older `transfer_transactions_consolidated.csv` and
also depended on the retired ownership data (`crop_plot_owner_WD.dta`), so it no longer ran. The repoint reproduces the
committed table content-identically. `swp_transfer_prices.csv` also has the 2010 Westside-5 strict-bilateral deal and
non-strict pool/banking/lease/contract rows — hence the year filter. The same panel supplies Fact 4's prose numbers
(\$342--\$1,271 range; volume-weighted means \$1,126/\$1,042 for 2014/2015; Buena Vista 17,836 AF = 65%), so table and
text stay consistent. See [[swp_delivery_entitlement_weighted]] (D10 transfer prices), [[ownership_analysis_dropped]].
